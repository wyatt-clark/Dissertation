\begin{dedication} 


This work is dedicated to three people that I love dearly.

{\raggedright
{\addtolength{\leftskip}{40mm} 
\vspace{12pt}
\emph{To my son, Aldaraa Anderson Clark;}

{\addtolength{\leftskip}{5mm} 
the apple of my eye,\\
%You have made me the epitome\\
from a doting father.\\
You bring more joy to my life\\
than you could ever know.\\
\par}

\vspace{12pt}
\emph{To my good friend, Jason Chaney; }

{\addtolength{\leftskip}{5mm} 
%who, at the cusp of adulthood,\\ 
%departed this world much too abruptly\\
%on a crisp autumn night.\\
I think of you often;\\
of windy country roads leading nowhere;\\
%of water flowing between stones piled 
%beneath the river birch's protective shade; 
and the appealing, yet impossible, prospect\\ 
of turning back time.\\
\par}
\vspace{12pt}
\emph{To my grandfather, George Anderson;}

{\addtolength{\leftskip}{5mm} 
the eternal tinkerer and\\
builder of windmills;\\
from whom I obtained both\\
my intellectual curiosity,\\
and desire to never stop learning.\\
\par}

}
}
\end{dedication}
